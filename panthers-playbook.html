3B Coach Signs
Indicator Touch the bill of the hat first — activates all signs below except steal and wipe-off.
Steal Touch any bare skin — no indicator needed. Skin = steal.
Bunt Indicator → touch the belt.
Delayed Steal Indicator → fold arms across chest (football ref delay-of-game signal).
Safety Squeeze Indicator → cup hands together several times.
Fake Bunt / Slash Indicator → touch cheek as if being slapped.
Hit & Run Indicator → both hands sweep from chest outward to shoulders.
Take Indicator → fist, straight arm down at side. Do not swing.
Wipe-Off Both hands wipe down shirt, chest to stomach — cancels previous sign. No indicator needed.
1B Coach Signs
Steal 2nd While talking to the runner at 1st, coach says the runner's jersey number out loud. Runner steals 2nd on next pitch.
Verbal sign only — sounds like normal coach chatter to everyone else.
First & Third Defense
Coach calls three numbers (e.g. 3, 6, 2). Only the first number matters — the rest are decoys.
COLOR
Pitcher picks off 3rd. 3B throws to 2nd if R1 breaks.
"3" first
Catcher throws directly to 3rd base.
"2" first
C throws to 2nd. 2B cuts → fires home.
"6" first — C throws to 2nd. Shortstop cuts → fires home.
Pickoff at 2nd — "Horns" Series
Setup Coach calls "Horns" → pitcher knows pickoff to 2nd is coming. Timing play.
SS Crashes Coach pats head → SS breaks hard to 2nd base.
2B Crashes Coach pats chest → 2B breaks hard to 2nd base.
Once infielder crashes → catcher drops glove → pitcher picks off to 2nd.
Bunt Defense Calls
Call the color before the pitch — all players react to their assignment.
⬛ Black — No runners / R on 1B
1B — Stay home
2B — Play position; rotate & back up 1B on past ball
SS — Cover 2B; read situation, maybe cover 3B
3B — Charge; do not field, cover 3B
C — Charge; 3B fields, cover 3B
P — Charge bunt
⬜ White — Lead runner on 2B
1B — Charge bunt
2B — Cover 1B; cheat over pre-pitch
SS — Cover 2B
3B — Cover 3B; ready for tag play
C — Charge bunt
P — Charge bunt
🟣 Purple — Wheel play, R on 2B
1B — Charge bunt
2B — Cover 1B; cheat over pre-pitch
SS — Cover 3B; ready for tag play
3B — Charge bunt
C — Charge bunt
P — Charge; deliver once SS breaks to 3B
"Let's Go Purple" (Pick Play): SS breaks to 3B as usual. 2B breaks to 2nd for a pickoff instead of covering 1B. Pitcher does an inside move to 2B.
